What to look for in a good budget headlamp

  • Lumens / light output — how bright it is at max and medium/low.
  • Battery type — rechargeable / AA/AAA / proprietary battery.
  • Water resistance — IPX rating, useful in rain or snow.
  • Comfort & weight — straps, balance, size.
  • Beam type — flood vs spot vs mixed.
  • Extra modes — red light, SOS, dimming, etc.
